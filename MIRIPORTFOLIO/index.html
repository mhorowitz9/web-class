I'm an enthusiastic and versatile student, double majoring in Communications Media and New Media Digital Design with a concentration in Art, Text, and Design. My journey spans a range of disciplines, including Digital Storytelling, Communications, Graphic Design, Journalism, UX/UI Design, Marketing, Computer Science, Website Development, and Editing. Throughout my experience in New Media Digital Design, I've actively participated in diverse projects, conducting interviews, creating surveys, and implementing solutions. My Graphic Design expertise integrates with broader communication skills, allowing me to craft visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. In Communications Media, I specialize in crafting narratives through scriptwriting and podcast editing, finding joy in shaping stories that captivate and establish meaningful connections. In this dynamic intersection of design and communications, I enjoy navigating the best of both worlds, fusing creativity with storytelling for impactful experiences.
