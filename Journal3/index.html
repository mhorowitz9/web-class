Throughout this class, I have learned to communicate and design my ideas with code. As a creative person, this enhanced my growth because it enabled me to experiment with interactivity for a website user. I am delighted with my work because each one sharpened my skills. In the future, I am excited to continue my learning because this is just the doorway to many opportunities, especially as a person who loves art and technology. Thank you very much for an excellent semester!
